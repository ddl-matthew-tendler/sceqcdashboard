AI Insights is an operational intelligence engine built into the SCE QC Hub that automatically detects patterns across QC activity data — surfacing where cycle time is lost, what's blocking progress, and where resources are misallocated. Each insight drills down to root causes with supporting charts and one-click remediation actions.
